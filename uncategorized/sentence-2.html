SET COVER and MAXIMUM COVERAGE are long known to be NP-hard [11]: not surprisingly, since the inherent complexity of multi-model fitting does not disappear by simply rephrasing it in different terms. Nevertheless, these optimization problems are among the oldest, most studied and widespread ones in the mathematical programming literature. Therefore we can reap the outcomes of the efforts made by the scientific community in addressing this issues, and enjoy the fruits of several studies focused on approximating the solutions of these problems.

众所周知，SET COVER和MAXIMUM COVERAGE是NP难的[11]：毫不奇怪，因为多模型拟合的固有复杂性并没有通过简单地用不同的术语重新表述而消失。 然而，这些优化问题是数学规划文献中最古老，研究最多和最普遍的问题之一。 因此，我们可以收获科学界在解决这一问题方面所做出的努力的成果，并享受几项研究的成果，这些研究的重点是近似解决这些问题。
